# IndieAppStack Content Planning

Use this skill when planning what IndieAppStack should publish next.

## Inputs To Gather

- Existing articles and their status.
- Categories, tools, and affiliate program status.
- Current business goal: authority, traffic, newsletter, affiliate revenue, or launch credibility.
- Any search, click, or newsletter data available.

## Planning Principles

- Lead with reader jobs: choose a backend, launch subscriptions, set up analytics, improve ASO, ship screenshots, catch crashes, launch a landing page.
- Mix article types: flagship guide, comparison, tool review, setup recipe, checklist, and refresh.
- Publish authority content before heavy monetization.
- Do not create a "best X" page unless the article has a concrete decision framework.
- Every article needs a visual plan.

## Topic Score

Score each topic from 1 to 5:

- Reader pain: how urgent and common the decision is.
- Search intent: how clearly the article can satisfy a query.
- Monetization fit: whether affiliate or partner links are natural.
- Evidence readiness: whether source checks, screenshots, examples, or hands-on notes can support the piece.
- Internal-link value: whether the article strengthens a category, tool page, comparison, or Stack Finder route.

## Output

Return:

- Publishing sprint.
- Topic table with scores and rationale.
- Required evidence and visuals.
- Suggested internal links.
- Affiliate status for each monetizable article.
