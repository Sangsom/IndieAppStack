# Weekly Review Checklist

Use every Friday.

## Content

- Which article got the most useful reader actions?
- Which article had traffic but weak internal movement?
- Which article needs a better intro, title, or table?
- Which article has stale pricing or product claims?
- Which topic should be moved from idea to brief?

## Affiliate

- Which `/go/[slug]` links were clicked?
- Which active affiliate link has no relevant article support?
- Which high-intent article has no approved affiliate program yet?
- Which partner applications need follow-up?
- Are disclosures visible on every monetized page?

## Publishing

- What is next week's flagship article?
- What supporting article will strengthen the cluster?
- What existing article will be refreshed?
- What visual asset is needed?
- What source checks are needed before publishing?
