# Affiliate And Partner Program Targets

Last reviewed: 2026-06-30.

## Principle

Apply for programs in the order that best matches reader value, not highest commission. Where a program is not clearly available, keep a direct link and use outreach only after the site has credible articles and traffic.

## Tier 1: Apply Or Start Conversation Early

| Tool       | Current Fit | Program Path                                                   | Notes                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ---------- | ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| RevenueCat | Very high   | [Direct partner program](https://www.revenuecat.com/partners/) | Official partner page mentions referral revenue, partner pricing, growth support, and certification. Strong audience fit for subscription app builders.                           |
| Framer     | Medium-high | [Creator/partner program](https://www.framer.com/creators)     | Official creator page mentions referral commission and subscription-based referral payouts. Best fit for landing page and template workflow articles.                             |

## Tier 2: Research And Apply After Initial Content Exists

| Tool Group       | Targets                                     | Why                                                                                                                                                         |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Paywalls         | Adapty, Superwall, Qonversion               | High buyer intent, natural comparison content, strong subscription audience fit.                                                                            |
| ASO              | AppTweak, Appfigures, AppFollow             | Clear buying-intent articles around keyword research, rankings, review monitoring, and launch workflows.                                                    |
| Analytics        | TelemetryDeck, PostHog, Amplitude, Mixpanel | Useful for event-tracking content; monetization depends on partner availability and audience size.                                                          |
| Screenshots      | Appscreens, Screenshots Pro                 | Strong fit for launch and App Store asset articles.                                                                                                         |
| Dev productivity | Codemagic, Bitrise, Emerge Tools            | Good fit for advanced builders once the site has technical credibility.                                                                                     |
| Landing pages    | Webflow                                     | Relevant for landing page articles, but current affiliate or partner availability should be verified from an official source before adding claims or links. |
| Email/waitlist   | beehiiv, Kit                                | Relevant for launch stack content; confirm whether creator or partner terms fit software/tool content.                                                      |

## Application Package

Prepare before applying:

- Site positioning: "A practical field guide for solo mobile developers choosing tools, building systems, and shipping durable apps."
- Audience: solo iOS, Android, Flutter, React Native, and web-to-app builders.
- Example content: 3 to 5 strong guides with source checks and custom visuals.
- Monetization policy: honest fit guidance, no commission-based rankings, visible affiliate disclosure.
- Tracking: `/go/[slug]` redirect clicks, Plausible events, and admin click analytics.

## Link Placement Rules

- Add affiliate links after program approval.
- Use direct non-affiliate links while program status is pending.
- Place links in decision moments: recommendation matrix, setup checklist, and final next steps.
- Avoid repeating affiliate buttons after every section.
- Keep tool page links available even when affiliate CTAs exist.
