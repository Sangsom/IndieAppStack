# Initial 90-Day Publishing Plan

## Strategy

Start with authority and decision value. The first 90 days should make the site feel useful to a solo mobile developer even before affiliate programs are approved.

Target cadence:

- 2 new articles per week.
- 1 meaningful refresh per week.
- 1 new or updated visual per article.
- 1 affiliate program application batch every two weeks.
- 1 analytics review every Friday.

## Image Requirements

Each article should include:

- One hero visual that communicates the article job.
- One decision table, workflow diagram, checklist graphic, or annotated screenshot.
- Alt text written for meaning, not keyword stuffing.
- Source notes for screenshots and any third-party marks.

## Refresh Targets

Refresh these seeded posts first:

- Best monetization tools for solo mobile developers.
- Supabase vs Firebase for indie mobile apps.
- RevenueCat vs Adapty for iOS subscriptions.
- Appfigures vs AppTweak for ASO research.
- Best landing page builders for mobile apps.

## Success Criteria

By day 90:

- 24 publishable articles or substantial refreshes.
- At least 8 articles with custom diagrams or screenshots.
- 8 to 12 affiliate or partner programs researched.
- 3 to 5 approved or active monetization relationships.
- Click tracking working for every active affiliate link.
- A repeatable weekly analytics review habit.
