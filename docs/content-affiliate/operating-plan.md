# Content Affiliate Operating Plan

Created: 2026-06-30.

IndieAppStack should become a trusted practical guide before it becomes an affiliate site. The site already has the right technical foundation: articles, topic queue, tool directory, affiliate programs, redirect links, compliance/disclosure, click analytics, AI drafts, Plausible, and Stack Finder. The next job is editorial discipline.

## Strategic Bet

Own a narrow, valuable reader:

> Solo mobile developers choosing tools for monetization, backend, analytics, ASO, launch, screenshots, crash reporting, and developer productivity.

The content should help this reader make real tradeoffs. Affiliate links should appear only where a tool is a natural next step.

## First 90 Days

- Publish or substantially refresh 24 pieces.
- Prioritize flagship guides and comparison pages with custom visuals.
- Apply for affiliate or partner programs only after the relevant articles are credible.
- Use `/go/[slug]` and visible disclosure for monetized links.
- Review analytics weekly and update the topic queue.

## Initial Publishing Mix

- 40% practical guides and setup recipes.
- 25% comparison pages.
- 20% tool reviews or tool clusters.
- 10% launch checklists and templates.
- 5% opinionated field notes or data-backed essays.

## First Articles To Prioritize

1. Subscription MVP stack for a solo iOS app.
2. RevenueCat vs Adapty vs Superwall.
3. Supabase vs Firebase vs Appwrite for mobile apps.
4. Crash reporting setup for a solo app launch.
5. ASO keyword research workflow for a first app.
6. Framer vs Webflow for indie app landing pages.
7. Email waitlist stack for a mobile app launch.
8. App screenshots workflow for non-designers.

## Affiliate Priority

Start with programs that match obvious buying-intent content:

- RevenueCat partner program.
- Framer creator/partner program.
- Appwrite partner program as a partner/outreach path rather than confirmed affiliate.
- Webflow should be researched next for landing page content, but do not treat its affiliate status as confirmed until an official source is verified.

Then research paywall, ASO, analytics, screenshot, CI/CD, and email tools.

## Quality Bar

Every article needs:

- Clear search intent.
- A short answer near the top.
- Decision framework or setup checklist.
- Internal links.
- Source-check dates recorded in `docs/content-affiliate/evidence-log.md` and the Notion Editorial Evidence Log.
- Image brief or actual custom visual tracked in `docs/content-affiliate/visual-production-workflow.md` and the Notion Visual Production Board.
- Human review notes.
- Disclosure if affiliate links are present or planned.

## Plugin Source

The project-owned plugin source lives at:

`plugins/indieappstack-content-affiliate`

It includes role agents, skills, and operating docs for content planning, article production, affiliate operations, and performance review.
